AIFCWebinar: Registrations are now closed

Association of Indian Football Coaches (AIFC) is set to kick-start a Webinar in Football Coaching. It will be held in 23 states across the country between 15th April to 8th May 2020. The webinar will comprise of eight modules. Coordinators have been appointed to each state with whom the participants can register their details with to be part of the webinar. Registrations were done by the appointed Coordinators in every state. As the current slots for every state has been met, registrations are now closed. For those that have registered, the respected coordinators from the states will keep you informed about further details. KINDLY NOTE: For further details, please follow AIFC on all social media platforms.
